About Us

Crash Coffee is a luxury coffee & goods company based in Toronto, Canada. Inspired by the Korean Tv Drama, ‘Crash Landing on You’, CEO Andrea Lau decided to open her own Coffee business in Toronto and wanted to focus more on the caffeinated-inspired drinks from South Korea. She recruited her best friends at Western University Kara Han and Rachel Quon to upon up the shop. Due to their immense popularity, the team decided that offering an online platform where local customers can order powdered mixes of their favourite drinks and specialty items to go or on delivery would give the customer the freedom to make their favourite cup of coffee or bake their favourite treats at home or in their free time!

Meet The Team

Andrea Lau, Chief Executive Officer

Born and raised in Toronto, Andrea has always been fascinated by the Coffee industry. Andrea studied Business and Computer science and has always dreamt of opening her own e-commerce business. In her free time, you’ll find her cooking up new recipes and listening to house music.
Born and raised in Vancouver, Kara met Andrea in their first year of university in 2018. Their shared interests include coffee and Shiba Inus, a beautiful dog which both girls have! In her free time, you’ll find Kara brushing up her programming skills or going on a shopping spree!
Also from Toronto, Rachel and Andrea met in their second year of university while taking a Computer Science class. Rachel loves designing and loves to read in her free time. She has a passion for interior design. Andrea appointed Rachel as the CFO due to her proven skills in data analytics.
